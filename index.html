About me

I'm a motivated graduate student from Iowa State University, with specialization in Robotics, Computer Vision, Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). I love making machines smarter by bridging the gap between simulation and reality, basically teaching them how the real world actually works.

My goal is to help organizations transform their operations through intelligent robotics solutions that not only work reliably in unpredictable environments, but also unlock new possibilities for innovation and efficiency. With my comprehensive background spanning design, simulation, fabrication, and control systems, I am driven to create automation that doesn't just replace human tasks, but amplifies human potential and delivers transformative results.
